Triangle Time!

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices.Triangles can be classified according to the lengths of their sides.They comprise of the equilateral,isosceles and scalene triangle.

Triangle Fact- longest side of a right angle triangle is called the hypotenuse, it is always found opposite the right angle.

  1. Equilateral Triangle- Has all sides the same length. Its angles also measure up to 60 degrees.
  2. Scalene Triangle- Has all its sides of different lengths. It has all unequal angles.
  3. Isosceles Triangles-Has two sides equal.It also has two equal angles.
